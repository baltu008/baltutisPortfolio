Experience and Motives

I began working at the age of 14, gaining professional leadership, interpersonal, and analytical qualities along the way. My work ethic and other qualities placed me in multiple diverse work settings where I built off of my pre-existing traits and discovered many more.

Furthermore, I thrive in an environment where I and co-workers build on each other's proficiencies, promoting personal cultivation in and outside of the work environment. My work experience ranges from hospitality, all the way to healthcare and now computer programming.

Currently, I am searching for a position where I can combine my extensive healthcare knowledge and my working proficiency as full-stack developer. I continue to strive to find challenging areas where my skills are put to the test.
